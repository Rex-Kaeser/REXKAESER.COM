About

For my MEGN 200 final project at the Colorado School of Mines, I tackled the challenge of building an alarm clock that would run away, making turning it off a challenge that would wake you up. Our solution was an alarm clock with wheels that would activate and run away, forcing the user to chase it down. I personally oversaw the electrical wiring and coding of the robot. The successful implementation of our solution allowed the robot to use LIDAR to actively scan its environment and run away on two wheels.
